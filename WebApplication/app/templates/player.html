About Player Analysis

Based on players behaviors, the team placement of a player can be rendered based on analyzing weights of differnet behaviours by regression method.
Skills are assigned with weighted according to their contributions to matches. A radar plot that shows players skill points is developed in this part, which is useful for players to discover their skills to be improved.
